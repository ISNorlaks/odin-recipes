Odin Recipes: Lasagna

Description

The ground beef lasagna recipe is a classic dish, much loved and very easy to prepare. Also known as Bolognese lasagna, due to its origins in Bologna, Italy, this recipe has conquered the world! This dish is a true celebration of flavors, with interspersed layers of pasta, ground meat sauce and melted cheese, resulting in a delicious and irresistible combination!

With simple ingredients such as ground beef, tomato sauce, cheese, ham and lasagna pasta, this recipe is a practical and delicious option for bringing the family together around the table!

Ingredients

  • 500g of lasagna pasta
  • 2 boxes of cream milk
  • 3 spoons of wheat flour
  • 500g of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 3 spoons of oil
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 500g minced meat
  • 3 butter spoons
  • 500g ham
  • salt: to your liking
  • 1 grated onion
  • 1 box of tomato sauce
  • 1 pack of grated cheese

How to prepare:

Cooking time: 50min

  1. Cook the paste using the instructions of the fabricant, then put it in a container with cold water and keep it close.
  2. Saut√© the garlic, onion, minced meat and tomato sauce, let it cook for 3min and keep it close.
  3. Melt the butter, put the 3 spoons of wheat flour and mix.
  4. Add the milk slowly and keep mixing.
  5. Lastly, add the milk cream, mix for 1min e turn off the heat.
  6. Pour part of the bolognese sauce in a container with half the pasta, half the ham, half the mozzarella cheese, all of the white sauce and then the rest of the pasta.
  7. Repeat the layers up to the border of the container.
  8. Finish with grated cheese and take to the high oven (220C), preheated, for about 20min.

That's it!, easy, isn't it?
